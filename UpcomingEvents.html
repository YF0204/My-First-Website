Upcoming Events

Here you will find the upcoming events schedulued at De Montfort University, be sure to take note of the time and date if you wish to attend any.

Friday 22nd April 2016 - Field Trip to Germany

There's still time to apply for the field trip to Germany. Students will be visiting various places and taking part in exciting activities such as the Berlin Wall, the Stadel Museum, the Neuschwanstein Castle and much more! If you are interested in going, please ask your personal tutor or course leader for an application form. Have fun!


Friday 29th April 2016 - Exam Revision Classes

On the 29th of April, revision classes for your exams will be starting. You should receive a message from your tutors telling you what rooms and exact times these will be taking place. As a fellow student, I highly recommend you go to these as you will need it. Good luck in your exams!

Wednesday 4th May 2016 - Technology Show 2016

At the DMU Technology Show, students will be given the chance to show their designs and creations to thousands of visitors including potential employers. This is a fantastic opportunity for the future of students, so please come and join us! You don't want to miss this event.
