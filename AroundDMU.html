Finding Your Way Around DMU

Where you first start here at DMU, you may not be sure where certain things are like the buildings for your lessons, where your lectures are or finding computers to work on.

Kimberlin Library

The Kimberlin Library is open 24/7 for all students, with a wide range of support and learning materials. These include group study areas, meeting rooms, textbooks and computers.

Gateway House

Gateway House is a six floor building, which has hundreds of computers with all the latest software for you to use.

Queens Building

The Queens Building will most likely be the location for your lectures. It's located next to the Kimberlin Library and contains massive rooms where your tutors will undertake the lectures.

DMU Campus Map

I have listed a map of the whole campus and provided the numbers of your buildings. You can also save the map by right-clicking on it and click save as. If you are not sure of how to find a certain building or area, please contact me.
