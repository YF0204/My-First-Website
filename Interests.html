My Computing Knowledge

Since I was at high school, I have always been interested in Computing and Technology. I studied GCSE IT, then I moved onto a level 2 and 3 apprenticeship. During this time, I became more confident and learnt how to create websites using Adobe Dreamweaver, word & spreadsheet processing using Microsoft Word & Excel, building PCs from scratch and so on. After completing my level 3 apprenticeship, I worked for a small company where I mainly had admin and basic IT duties. However, I noticed after a while that this work was just not for me, therefore I decided to apply for university to enhance my skills and find a more better and interesting job.
